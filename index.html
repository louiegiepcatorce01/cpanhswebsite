Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll my child at CPANHS? +
Visit our Admissions office during office hours with the required documents: Birth Certificate, Latest Report Card from Previous School, and 1-2 Valid ID's.
What are the school hours? +
Classes run from Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM. The school office is open from 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM.
What programs are offered at CPANHS? +
We offer Junior High School (Grades 7-10) and Senior High School (Grades 11-12) programs with various academic tracks.
How can I contact the school administration? +
You can reach us through our contact page, visit our office during business hours, or call our main office number.
